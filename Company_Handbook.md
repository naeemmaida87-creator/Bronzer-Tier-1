# Company Handbook - Rules of Engagement

## Communication Rules
- Emails mein hamesha polite tone use karo
- Naye contacts ko reply karne se pehle approval lo
- 100 dollar se zyada koi bhi payment automatic mat karo

## Priority Keywords
- urgent, asap, invoice, payment, help

## Auto-Approve Allowed
- Known contacts ko email reply
- Files create aur read karna
- Dashboard update karna

## Always Ask Human First
- Koi bhi payment
- Naye contacts
- Files delete karna
